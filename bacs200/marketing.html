Faber-Castell

Faber-Castell is one of the most prominant art supply companies in the market. They are known as the top pencil manufacturer in the world and take sustanability seriously in order to run a successful business while also keeping in mind how production of their products affects the environment.

Business Success

Faber-Castell has been manufacturing high quality products since 1761. They produce high-quality products for writing, drawing creative design, and decorative cosmetics. They also have a world wide production capacity of over 2000 million wooden-cased pencils. This historic german company is a go-to for artists and consumers across the globe. They are known for making some of the highest quality art supplies that you can purchase and help foster the creativity of individuals everywhere. They have even expanded into making products that are more kid friendly for younger artists!

Sustainability

Faber-Castell has always had a constant focus on sustainability. Here are some fun sustainable facts about their company!

  • Faber-Castell’s own forests in Prata, south-east Brazil, capture 900,000 tonnes of carbon dioxide (CO2). This was confirmed by TÜV Rheinland® in 2012. The 10,000 hectares of forests therefore not only secure our sustainable wood needs, but also help decrease the burden on the atmosphere through photosynthesis, in which CO2 is converted into biomass.
  • One third of the company's own forests remain untouched and provide habitats for around 660 indigenous animal and plant species
  • They sustainably manage 10,000 hectares of its own pine forests. These provide the raw material for our pencils and cover 86 percent of our worldwide wood demand.
  • Every year, around 300,000 seedlings are planted in the company's own forests. We are growing one truckload of wood per hour.
  • Faber-Castell covers 82 percent of the global energy demand from renewable sources.
